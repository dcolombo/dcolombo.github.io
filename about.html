About me

Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the Max Planck Institute for Radio Astronomy (Bonn, Germany) since 2015. Between 2014-2015, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Alberta (Edmonton, Canada).

Education

I obtained my PhD from the Heidelberg University (Heidelberg, Germany). For my PhD, I worked at the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy on the "Gas organization in M51 - The impact of spiral arm dynamics on Giant Molecular Cloud properties" topic. I obtained my MSc in Astrophysics and Space Physics and BSc in Physics from the University of Milano-Bicocca (Milano, Italy).

Research interests

Molecular clouds, Milky Way structure, galaxy evolution, star formation quenching, single-dish and interformetric mm/cm observations, machine learning.
